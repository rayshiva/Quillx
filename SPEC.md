# Quillx Specification v3.0

## What is Quillx?

**Quillx** is an open, self-declared standard for disclosing the level of AI involvement in a software project — expressed through the language of writing and authorship.

Because code is literature. Every function has a voice. Every commit tells a story.

Score your project from **1 to 5**.

---

## The Scale

| Score | Label        | What it means                                                                                     |
|-------|--------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1     | Verse        | Every line deliberate. Human-authored entirely. Crafted like poetry — nothing accidental, nothing wasted. |
| 2     | Prose        | Structured and readable. AI assisted with repetition or scaffolding, but the human voice is unmistakable throughout. |
| 3     | Adapted      | A collaboration. Human-directed, AI-generated, meaningfully shaped and edited — like adapting a screenplay from a novel. |
| 4     | Ghostwritten | AI did the writing. You provided the brief, reviewed the output, and signed off. The ideas are yours. The words, not so much. |
| 5     | Lorem Ipsum  | Placeholder shipped as product. AI generated it, nobody read it closely, and it went out the door. |

---

## Scoring Guidelines

Ask yourself:

- **Who chose the structure?** — Human-designed architecture → lower score.
- **Who wrote the logic?** — Human-authored core logic → lower score.
- **Was the output read line by line?** — Deep review → lower score.
- **Were tests written intentionally?** — Human-crafted tests → lower score.
- **Does the code have a recognisable voice?** — If yes, probably a 1 or 2.

**Score = your honest best estimate. No auditing. Your reputation is the enforcement.**

## How to Use

### In your README

```markdown
[![Quillx](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/qainsights/quillx/main/badges/quillx-2.svg)](https://github.com/qainsights/quillx)
```

Replace `2` with your project's score (1–5).

### In plain text

> **Quillx: 2/5 · Prose** — Architecture and logic are mine. AI scaffolded the boilerplate and tests.

---

## Principles

1. **Transparency over purity** — Not a competition. Honest disclosure.
2. **Spectrum over binary** — AI use is a gradient, not a switch.
3. **Self-declared** — Trust the author. The community validates over time.
4. **Version your score** — Projects evolve. Scores can too.

---

## Why writing?

Code is the closest thing engineering has to literature. It has style. It has voice. It can be elegant or clumsy, precise or verbose. A codebase written with intent reads differently from one that was generated and accepted without scrutiny.

The writing metaphor isn't decorative. It's accurate.
